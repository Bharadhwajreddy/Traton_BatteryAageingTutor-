## 2. Role in the Schmalstieg 2014 model

This paper is the **experimental companion** to Schmalstieg et al. 2014. While Schmalstieg 2014 derives the holistic model and couples it to the electrothermal simulator, Ecker et al. 2014 reports the raw ageing data in detail: per-condition fade curves, degradation mode analysis (ICA/DVA), and discussion of observed phenomena like sudden death. Fitting the Schmalstieg model properly requires this dataset.

## 3. Key experimental findings

- **Calendar ageing**: activation energies 47–60 kJ/mol; strong SoC dependence especially near voltage plateaus on the graphite anode; t^n dependence with n between 0.5 and 1 (closer to 0.75)
- **Cycle ageing**: linear DoD dependence of fade coefficient per Ah; U-shaped mean-SoC dependence with minimum ≈ 50 % SoC; ~8500 FEC projected at 5 % DoD vs ~440 FEC at 100 % DoD
- **Sudden death**: random cell failure 250–950 cycles at identical conditions — not explained by any smooth parametric model
- **Resistance**: proportional to capacity fade; ~150 % of initial R₀ at sudden EOL
